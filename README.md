# Cosmetic Insights: Navigating Cosmetics Trends and Consumer Behavior with Tableau

An end-to-end Tableau analytics project that turns a cosmetics dataset into actionable, real-time insights for brand managers, analysts, and regulatory stakeholders — embedded into a Flask web application.

---

## 📌 Overview

The cosmetics industry is one of the fastest-growing and most competitive sectors, driven by changing consumer preferences, diverse skin types, brand innovation, and evolving pricing strategies. **Cosmetic Insights** analyzes a cosmetics dataset — brand, product category, price, rank, and skin-type suitability (Dry, Oily, Combination, Normal, Sensitive) — to uncover meaningful patterns and help businesses:

- Identify high-performing products
- Target specific customer segments
- Optimize marketing and product strategies

### Business Scenarios Covered
1. **Monitoring Consumer Preferences** — Detect and respond to shifts in interest for products/ingredients in real time.
2. **Addressing Product Concerns** — Track negative reviews or safety issues and enable swift quality-control response.
3. **Predictive Analysis & Product Innovation** — Use historical trends to anticipate demand and guide new product development.
