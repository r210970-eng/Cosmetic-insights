# Cosmetic Insights: Navigating Cosmetics Trends and Consumer Insights with Tableau

An interactive Tableau dashboard that helps cosmetics brand managers, analysts, and
leadership understand market trends and consumer behavior by connecting brand,
category, price, rank, and skin-type data (Dry, Oily, Combination, Normal, Sensitive)
in one filterable view.

## What's Inside This Dashboard

- **Brand & Pricing Analysis** — compare brand performance by price and rank
- **Skin-Type Segmentation** — filter and view product coverage across skin types
- **Interactive Filters & Drill-down** — from category summaries to individual products
- **Role-Based Access** — separate Analyst and Leadership views
- **Trend & Insight Reporting** — trend charts across categories and price bands
- **Export & Reporting** — download dashboard views as PDF/image for review meetings
